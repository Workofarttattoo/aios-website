‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Community Edition?
Clone from GitHub, run the installer script, or pull the Docker image. No credit card required. Full documentation at docs.aios.is
Can I try Professional before paying?
Yes! We offer 14-day free trials for Professional edition. No credit card required for trial.
What's included in the Professional tier?
All 8 meta-agents, ML/Quantum ML algorithms, 3 security tools, priority email support, and advanced customization options. Perfect for engineering teams.
Can I self-host instead of cloud?
Absolutely! Community and Professional editions are fully self-hosted on your infrastructure. Enterprise edition supports on-premises deployment with additional SLA guarantees.
What about GPU acceleration?
Ai:oS automatically detects and uses NVIDIA CUDA GPUs when available. This provides 2-5x speedup for ML algorithms and quantum simulations. Optional but highly recommended.
Can I upgrade later?
Yes, upgrades are instant and pro-rated. Start free, upgrade to Professional when you're ready. Can add modules individually or purchase bundles at a discount.
